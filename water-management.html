Water Conservation Tips

Drip Irrigation

Use drip irrigation systems to deliver water directly to plant roots, reducing water waste by up to 50%.

  • • Install drip emitters near plant base
  • • Use timers for automated watering
  • • Regular maintenance prevents clogging
  • • Adjust flow rates based on crop needs

Mulching

Apply organic mulch around plants to retain soil moisture and reduce evaporation.

  • • Use straw, leaves, or grass clippings
  • • Apply 2-4 inches thick layer
  • • Keep mulch away from plant stems
  • • Refresh mulch seasonally

Optimal Timing

Water during early morning or late evening to minimize evaporation losses.

  • • Best time: 6-8 AM or 6-8 PM
  • • Avoid midday watering
  • • Check soil moisture before watering
  • • Adjust frequency based on weather

Water Recycling

Collect and reuse rainwater and greywater for irrigation purposes.

  • • Install rainwater harvesting systems
  • • Use treated greywater for irrigation
  • • Store water in covered tanks
  • • Filter water before use
